As Indonesia's main tourist destination, Bali is well known for its refined arts, such as modern and traditional dances, sacred temples and pristine natural beauty. The nightlife is another aspect that travellers love! Spending time outdoors is customary in Bali, so remember to bring sunscreen! Bali, the famed Island of the Gods, with its varied landscape of hills and mountains, rugged coastlines and sandy beaches, lush rice terraces and barren volcanic hillsides all providing a picturesque backdrop to its colourful, deeply spiritual and unique culture, stakes a serious claim to be paradise on earth.
